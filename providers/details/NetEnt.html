                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          NetEnt History

The father and his partner Rolf Lundström founded the legendary AB Restaurang Rouletter in 1963 and signed a partnership deal with AB Roulett konsult & Spelautomater five years later.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          This partnership grew rapidly and in 1972 was registered Cherryföretagen (Cherry), a company that has become a leading gaming operator in the country. It stayed that way for 40 years and is still active in Sweden. In 1996, Pontus Lindwall founded NetEnt, and when the business went online, the studio became a pioneer and one of the biggest digital entertainment companies worldwide.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          In 2007, NetEnt AB became part of NGM Equity and in 2009, it moved to the Stockholm Stock Exchange main list. Meanwhile, the studio continued to develop unique video slots and RNG table games and climb up a steep ladder in competition with other top developers, like Microgaming, now Games Global, and Playtech.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          In 2020, NetEnt was acquired by another Swedish iGaming giant - Evolution Gaming Group AB. Until that moment, it was known as a pioneering developer of live casino software, so this move was shocking for the industry. The deal was for SEK 19.6 billion or nearly GBP 1.5 billion according to the exchange rates then.
